Table 1 - Data to be collected by PIRCS archive

(a) REFERENCE

• Latitude of each point on the horizontal grid used to report model output

• Longitude of each point on the horizontal grid used to report model output

• The vertical levels used to report model output.

• Two-dimensional field giving the terrain height in meters above mean sea level.

• Two-dimensional field giving the land fraction of each grid cell.


(b) THREE-HOURLY

Model output reported at 00,03,06 ..., 21 UTC. For fluxes, the output is a three-hour accumulation (e.g. output at 12 UTC is accumulated from 9 UTC- 12 UTC). For mass and temperature, output is the value at the reporting time. Values are reported on the horizontal grid of the participating model.

All fluxes are positive downward.

(b1) Surface fluxes

• incident solar (shortwave) radiation

• reflected solar (shortwave) radiation

• upward terrestrial (longwave) radiation

• downward terrestrial (longwave) radiation

• sensible heat flux

• latent heat flux (evapotranspiration)

• heat flux from the atmosphere into the model domain below the atmosphere

• convective precipitation

• stable precipitation

• momentum flux into the ground: E-W component

• momentum flux into the ground: N-S component
(b2) Top-of-atmosphere fluxes

• net solar (shortwave) radiation

• upward terrestrial (longwave) radiation

(b3) Mass

• surface pressure

• sea level pressure

• precipitable water (water vapor only)

(b4) Temperature

• surface air temperature (2 m level)

• skin temperature

(c) SIX-HOURLY

Atmospheric output sampled at 00, 06, 12, and 18 UTC. Output reported on the three-dimensional grid of the participating model.

• Temperature

• Specific Humidity

• Zonal (east-west) wind

• Meridonal (north-south) wind

• Geopotential height

• Pressure

(d) DAILY

Output reported on the horizontal grid of the participating model.

(d1) Air temperature at 2 meter elevation

• Daily maximum (period: 00 UTC - 00 UTC)

• Time of daily maximum

• Daily minimum (period: 0 UTC - 0 UTC)

• Time of daily minimum

(d2) Soil moisture

• Total amount of water in the soil column at 12 UTC

(d3) Runoff

• Accumulation over prior 24 hours (period: 0 UTC - 0 UTC)
