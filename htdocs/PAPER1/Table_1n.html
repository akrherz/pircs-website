(b) THREE-HOURLY

Model output reported at 00,03,06 ..., 21 UTC. For fluxes, the output is a three-hour accumulation (e.g. output at 12 UTC is accumulated from 9 UTC- 12 UTC). For mass and temperature, output is the value at the reporting time. Values are reported on the horizontal grid of the participating model.

All fluxes are positive downward.
