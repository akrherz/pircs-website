Table 1 - Data to be collected by PIRCS archive




THREE-HOURLY

Model output reported at 00,03,06 ..., 21 UTC. For fluxes, the output is a three-hour accumulation (e.g. output at 12 UTC is accumulated from 9 UTC- 12 UTC). All fluxes are positive downward. For mass and temperature, output is the value at the reporting time. Values are reported on the horizontal grid of the participating model.

ID Variable Unit
1. Surface fluxes ---
(a) incident solar (shortwave) radiation Joules m-2
(b) reflected solar (shortwave) radiation Joules m-2
(c) upward terrestrial (longwave) radiation Joules m-2
(d) downward terrestrial (longwave) radiation Joules m-2
(e) sensible heat flux Joules m-2
(f) latent heat flux (evapotranspiration) Joules m-2
(g) heat flux from the atmosphere into the model domain below the atmosphere Joules m-2
(h) convective precipitation mm
(i) stable precipitation mm
(j) momentum flux into the ground (E-W and N-S components separately) Joules m-2
2. Top-of-atmosphere fluxes ---
(a) net solar (shortwave) radiation Joules m-2
(b) upward terrestrial (longwave) radiation Joules m-2
3. Mass ---
(a) surface pressure Pa
(b) sea level pressure Pa
(c) precipitable water (water vapor only) mm
4. Temperature ---
(a) surface air temperature (2 m level) K
(b) skin temperature K




SIX-HOURLY

Atmospheric output sampled at 00, 06, 12, and 18 UTC. Output reported on the three-dimensional grid of the participating model.

ID Variable Units
1. Temperature K
2. Specific Humidity kg kg-1
3. Zonal (east-west) wind m s-1
4. Meridonal (north-south) wind m s-1
5. Geopotential height m
6. Pressure Pa

NOTE: Horizontal wind directions are with respect to the earth's latitude-longitude grid.




DAILY

Output reported on the horizontal grid of the participating model.

ID Variable Unit
1. Air temperature at 2 meter elevation ---
(a) Daily maximum and time of maximum (period: 0 (See Note) - 0 (See Note)) K, (See Note)
(b) Daily minimum and time of minimum (period: 0 (See Note) - 0 (See Note)) K, (See Note)
2. Soil moisture ---
--- Total amount of water in the soil column at 12 UTC mm
3. Runoff ---
--- Accumulation over prior 24 hours (period: 0 UTC - 0 UTC) mm

NOTE: Times of min/max should be reported in seconds after 00 UTC.



REFERENCE

1. Latitude and longitude of each point on the model's horizontal grid reported in the same order as the three-hourly output.
2. The model's vertical coordinate grid
