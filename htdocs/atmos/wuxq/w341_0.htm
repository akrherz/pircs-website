Course Objectives:

 

This course will be concerned with atmospheric thermodynamics, hydrostatic equilibrium and stability, with emphasis on understanding the basic concepts and physical processes.  Cloud physics will be introduced in the last week of the semester and continued in Meteorology 342. Building a solid foundation in physical principles becomes more important for interpreting increasingly complicated numerical models with a wide range of scales.
