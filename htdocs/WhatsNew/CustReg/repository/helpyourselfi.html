`Information About You and Your Company'

Selected Input Field Lengths

Please note that on some input fields the entry window scrolls to accomodate additional characters. You will be able to enter additional information until you hear a beep which indicates that you have reached the maximum allowable input for that field.

`Information About Your Company'

Self-Employed - If you are self-employed, click on this button. If you have a name for your company, enter your official company name in the `Full Company Name' field. If you do not provide a company name, we will use your first and last name for the company name.

Full Company Name - Enter your official company name.

Division/Department and Street Address Information - Enter your complete company mailing address. If you have a division or department name in your address, place this on the first line, followed by the street address on the next line(s). If you do not have a division or department, begin entering the street address on the first line. Do not use abbreviations unless necessary.

City - Please do not abbreviate the name of your city unless it is too long for the city field.

State/Prov - Use the standard post office abbreviations for your state or province.

`Information About You'

Salutation - This is an optional title. Examples are: Mr., Mrs., Ms., Dr., Prof., etc.

E-mail Address - Please enter the COMPLETE address to be used for E-mail sent to you from outside your company.
