VII. Summary
  • * Recent advances in computer hardware allow for much wider use of models for agroclimatic applications

  • * Models can be a significant tool to empower scientists from developing and developed countries to enhance their use of agroclimatic databases for a more efficient and sustainable agriculture

  • * The International Institute for Theoretical and Applied Physics at Iowa State University has a mission to train scientists from developing countries in state-of-the-art tools for basic and applied physics problems including modeling and analysis with agroclimatic databases
