Portland Compiler Information
 Environmental setup
for csh/tcsh
setenv PGI /usr/local/pgi
setenv PATH $PGI/linux86/bin:$PATH
or simply add $PGI/linux86/bin to your PATH line in your .cshrc

for sh/bash
export PGI=/usr/local/pgi
export PATH=$PGI/linux86/bin:$PATH
or simply add $PGI/linux86/bin to your PATH line in your .bash_profile or .bashrc file
