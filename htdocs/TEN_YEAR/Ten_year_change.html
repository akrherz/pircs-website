Climate Changes

Climate changes from two simulations by regional climate models (HIRHAM and RegCM2) driven by HadCM2 for 10-year periods. The model domain is the same as used by the Project to Intercompare Regional Climate Simulations (PIRCS).

Climate changes are differences between simulations using the HadCM2 scenario and control climates. The scenario period corresponds roughly to 2040-2050. The changes by HIRHAM and RegCM2 correspond roughly to differences between the 2040's and the 1990's. For HadCM2 figures, the differences are (2040-2069) minus (1961-1990).

PLEASE NOTE:

Results linked to this page are preliminary. They should not be discussed, cited or otherwise referenced without consulting DMI and ISU modelers who produced these results.
