PIRCS Meeting

12 January 1999, 7:30 pm

Dallas, Texas

The next PIRCS meeting will be held at the 1999 Annual Meeting of the American Meteorological Society in Dallas, Texas on Tuesday, January 12. We will meet in the Batik B conference room at the Wyndam Anatole hotel (the AMS conference HQ hotel). A buffet dinner will be available at 7:30 pm, with formal business starting at 8 pm. The start of the meeting has been pushed back to avoid overlap with a GCIP meeting called by Rick Lawford.

Attendance is limited to PIRCS participants and invited guests. If you have questions or would like to suggest agenda items for the meeting, please email the project coordinators at pircs@iastate.edu.
