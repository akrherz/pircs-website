DARLAM precipitation

Below this paragraph are plots of the 60-day accumulated precipitation for an observed data set and DARLAM, respectively. The precipitation from DARLAM is positioned slightly north of and has a slightly greater maximum than the observations. Many other models predict the heaviest precipitation north of the observations as well.
